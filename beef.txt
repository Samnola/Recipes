- 1 can of black beans 
- 1 can of kidney beans 
- 1 can diced tomatoes 
- 1/2 onion 
- 2 cloves garlic (minced)[
- 1/2 bell pepper 
- 1 tbsp olive oil

Seasoning: 
- 1 tsp chili powder 
- 1/2 tsp cumin 
- 1/2 tsp paprika 
- salt & pepper 

Instructions
1. Heat oil in a pan 
2. Cook onion + garlic until soft 
3. Add bell pepper cook 2-3 min
4. Add beans + tomatoes 
5. Add all seasoning 
6. Stir + let simmer for 15-20 min
